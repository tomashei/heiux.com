Master Thesis

Sea of Spores
Multifunctional Hall in Istanbul

My Role

Student

Location

Istanbul, Turkey

Design Area

10 000 m²

Supervisor

prof. Piotr Marciniak

Situation:


  • The author was wondering, were parametric tools only interesting innovation or they could be successfully applied in the design process to save time and money.

A perspective presenting complex layers and functions inside the building.

Task:


  • to prepare a computer program that would generate an entire building;
  • to find out whether the parametric design could have a positive economic impact on a company;
  • to prepare a tool-base that could be used in the future doctoral dissertation;
  • to design conception of the building that would respect local genius loci and provide a conception of the most modern sustainable solutions;

A bird-view presenting proposed building in landscape.

Action:


  • Research concerning location was conducted and genius loci deeply understood.
  • A parametric tool-set was chosen and a research schedule was prepared.
  • The draft conception of the building was proposed and then developed.
  • Genetic algorithms were used to optimize the variable conceptions of the building.

The complex Grasshopper script used to generate entire 3D model of the building.

Result:


  • The whole geometry of the building was designed to be a computer program.
  • Each part of the building could be easily controlled and changed in just a few moments.
  • A wide spectrum of diﬀerent designs could be very quickly delivered and compared.
  • According to input parameters of diﬀerent designs, materials usage and construction costs could be quickly estimated.

Final posters presented to jury during the final submission.
