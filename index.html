Framework for Innovation

    When designing digital products today, it is both impossible and unsensible to set up a rigid process and follow it in every situation. We need to be agile and quick to adapt to the ever-changing market situation.

    However, if you did things randomly, you would always get random results. It is why I use the Double Diamond Framework for Innovation: an agile and lightweight design process. Before I start any new job, together with a client, we firstly perform comprehensive research and decide the appropriate tool-set. The Double Diamond is not dogma, just a starting point...


    Every day I strive to improve my workflow, learning new methodologies and broadening my knowledge of usability heuristics and psychology.
